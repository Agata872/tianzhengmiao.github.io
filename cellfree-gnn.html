Cell-Free Massive MIMO

GNN-based Precoding in Cell-free Massive MIMO

Cell-free massive MIMO aims to provide uniform high-quality wireless service via distributed Access Points (APs). This project proposes a GNN-based precoding framework trained on synthetic CSI and fine-tuned using real-world measurements from our Techtile testbed.
