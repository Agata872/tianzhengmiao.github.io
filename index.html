About Me

I am currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with the Department of Electrical Engineering (ESAT), Katholieke Universiteit Leuven, under the supervision of Prof. François Rottenberg. Prior to this, I received the B.Eng. degree in Telecommunication Engineering from the Xidian University, Xi'an, China in 2022. And I obtained the M.Phil. degree in Computer and Information Engineering from the Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, China, in 2024, under the supervisions of Prof. Rui Zhang.

As part of the EMPOWER-6G project, my research will focus on designing and implementing PHY mechanisms to enable CF networking based on real channel data acquired in the KU Leuven testbed. And I will also study dynamic algorithms for selecting the optimal cooperation levels between DU and AP to reduce the amount of interference at the regional edge while simultaneously keeping the computational complexity at the CPU within reasonable bounds.
